Find Your OAuth 2.0 Client

What you need to do:

  1. You should now be on the "Credentials" page
  2. Scroll down until you see a section called "OAuth 2.0 Client IDs"
  3. In that section, you'll see a table with your OAuth clients listed
  4. Find the one you created for this app (it might be called "Web client" or have a custom name you gave it)
  5. Click on the NAME of the OAuth client (the blue/underlined text, NOT the pencil icon)

Add Authorized JavaScript Origins

What you need to do:

  1. After clicking your OAuth client name, a new page will open with details
  2. Scroll down until you see "Authorized JavaScript origins" section
  3. Click the blue button that says "+ ADD URI"
  4. A new text box will appear
  5. Copy the URL from the box below (click the green Copy button)
  6. Paste it into the text box in Google Cloud Console
  7. Press Enter or click outside the box to confirm
